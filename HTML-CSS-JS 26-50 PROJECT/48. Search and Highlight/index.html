Selenophiles are individuals who have a deep love and fascination for the moon. This term, derived from the Greek words "selene" (moon) and "philos" (loving), describes those who find joy and inspiration in the moon's phases, its serene glow, and its presence in the night sky. Selenophiles often appreciate the moon for its beauty and its influence on tides, culture, and human emotions. They may engage in activities like moon-gazing, photography, or even studying lunar science. This lunar affinity can evoke a sense of peace, wonder, and a connection to the cosmos.
